About RoadScan

Welcome to RoadScan, your comprehensive solution for monitoring and improving road cleanliness in Angeles City, Philippines. At RoadScan, we believe in leveraging technology to create a smarter, cleaner, and more connected urban environment.

Our Mission

Our mission is to enhance the quality of city living by harnessing the power of traffic cameras to monitor road conditions and classify cleanliness. Through innovative image classification algorithms, we provide real-time insights into the cleanliness of roads, helping to create a safer and more pleasant commuting experience for everyone.

How It Works

RoadScan utilizes a network of strategically placed traffic cameras across Angeles City. These cameras capture images of the roadways, and our advanced image recognition technology analyzes these images to determine the cleanliness of the roads. The results are then presented on our interactive map, allowing users to stay informed about the state of roads in different areas.

Join Us In Building a Cleaner City

RoadScan is more than just a website; it's a community-driven initiative. We invite you to explore the site, stay informed, and actively participate in keeping our city streets clean. Together, we can make a positive impact on the quality of life for all residents. Thank you for being a part of the RoadScan community. Let's pave the way to cleaner and safer roads, one image at a time.
